History of My Paradise Living Hotel

Paradise Living Hotel was officially established on 21 May 2007 with a vision to provide a peaceful and relaxing escape for travelers from around the world. Located in a serene and picturesque setting, the hotel started as a small, family-run business with a strong commitment to comfort, quality service, and island hospitality.

Over the years, Paradise Living Hotel has grown into a well-loved destination, known for its warm atmosphere, personalized care, and attention to detail. Through dedication and continuous improvement, the hotel has expanded its services and facilities to meet the evolving needs of guests, while still maintaining the charm and tranquility that inspired its creation.

Today, Paradise Living Hotel stands as a symbol of relaxation and excellence, welcoming guests with open arms and offering a true taste of paradise.
